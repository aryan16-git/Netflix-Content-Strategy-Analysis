KEY FINDINGS
------------

1. Content Library Composition
   - Total Library: 8,793 titles
   - Mix: 69.7% Movies (6,129 titles) | 30.3% TV Shows (2,664 titles)
   - Insight: Netflix is primarily a movie-focused platform with a 70/30 split

2. Genre Strategy
   - Top Genres: International Movies, Dramas, Comedies
   - Genre Diversity: 42 unique genre categories
   - Insight: Extensive genre categorization enables personalized recommendations

3. Geographic Presence
   - Countries Represented: 85 nations
   - US Share: 45.9% of total content
   - Top International Markets: India, United Kingdom, Canada
   - Insight: Strong US base with growing international representation

4. Growth Patterns
   - Timeline: 2008 to 2021 (14 years)
   - Growth Rate: 6172% increase post-2016
   - Peak Year: 2019 with 2016 titles
   - Insight: Aggressive expansion strategy since 2016
